Private tastings with the owners or winemaker also by appointment.
First planted in 1973, Ruby Vineyard has some of the oldest vines planted in the rich Laurelwood soil. Ruby continues to be one of the best hidden gems in the Willamette Valley, offering some of the finest Pinot Noir in the region.
A family owned and operated vineyard and winery, you can usually find the owners Steve Hendricks or Flora Habibi on the grounds. Come in and stop by to talk to them about the harvest, learn about the wine club, or just enjoy a glass on our deck.
